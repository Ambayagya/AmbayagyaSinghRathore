about.

I'm a developer & designer based in Jaipur, India.

I began learning coding in 2020 since I'd always wanted to create my own applications and websites. But I knew nothing about coding at the time, so I began looking for it on the internet, and fortunately, I discovered a free Google course for developing Android apps, so I enrolled in it. That same day, I made a one-page app that merely displays my name on the screen, but it made me very pleased. That's when I fell in love with coding. Since then, I've learnt python programming, front-end development, and (to a lesser extent) android app development through tonnes of online tutorials, classes, and a lot of hard work. I'm currently exploring a variety of different fields in IT industry.

When I'm not in front of my computer, you'll find me playing with dogs (yeah, I love dogs), watching films, working out in the gym (sometimes in the park), sleeping on my bed or hanging out with friends and family.
